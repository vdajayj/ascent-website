About the Conference

India's aspirations for achieving a sustainable and self-reliant future are intrinsically tied to advancements in chemical engineering and nanotechnology. These disciplines are essential for addressing critical challenges in renewable energy, green manufacturing, healthcare, and environmental sustainability. Aligned with national goals such as achieving net-zero emissions by 2070, promoting a circular economy, and enhancing India’s Sustainability Development Index.

This conference invites researchers, scientists, innovators, and startups from academia, research institutions, and industry R&D to engage in thought-provoking discussions, showcase pioneering innovations, and explore cutting-edge research areas. Special emphasis will be placed on empowering young researchers and students to contribute to India’s sustainable future.

ASCENT 2025 aims to unite India’s brightest minds and passionate innovators to explore transformative technologies, advance national sustainability goals, and shape a resilient and sustainable future. Together, let us rise to new heights in chemical engineering and nanotechnology, contributing to India’s journey toward global leadership in sustainable innovation. ASCENT 2025 will provide a platform to foster the next generation of scientific leaders.

We invite researchers, scientists, young innovators, and students to submit abstracts not exceeding 250 words in the prescribed format through the provided Google Form. At least one author or co-author must register to present the paper at the conference. The abstract should align with the conference themes and demonstrate originality, innovation, and relevance.
